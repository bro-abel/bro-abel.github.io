Renewable Energy Solutions

LIBANGO provides Solar and Battery Energy Storage Systems for residential, commercial, and industrial customers. These solutions are used as backup power for uninterrupted supply, as primary off-grid power, and as tertiary emergency power. Options include grid-tied and off-grid systems powered by solar, wind, and advanced energy storage technologies.

LIBANGO partners with international suppliers for system engineering and quality control, while its local technical staff handles installation and support. Notable deployments include battery backup systems for critical datacenter infrastructure such as LIBTELCO and Cable Consortium of Liberia (CCL).

Products and Services

  • Solar PV power systems
  • Battery Energy Storage Systems (ESS) for industrial-scale UPS
  • Wind and hybrid wind-solar power systems
  • Maintenance and support services
