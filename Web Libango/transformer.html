Power Transformer Products and Services

LIBANGO supplies transformers of various sizes, poles, and connection materials. Through its acquisition of Transformer Manufacturing Services (TMS),
LIBANGO also manufactures and refurbishes transformers and provides monitoring solutions for utilities and large customers.

Services Include

  • Sale of New Transformers
  • Repair and Refurbishment of Transformers
  • Removal and Installation of Transformers
  • Cabling and Construction of Substations
  • Transformer Maintenance Services

With deep experience under TMS, LIBANGO can refurbish any damaged electricity transformer and restore it to operation within 7 days.
All refurbished units come with a 12-month defect warranty backed by our quality assurance process.

Company Experience

  • Over 50 years of combined experience in transformer manufacturing, assembly, installation, and refurbishment
  • Refurbishment services delivered in the Philippines, Ghana, and across Africa
  • More than 5,000 transformers refurbished globally (15kVA to 2500kVA)
  • Over 500 transformers refurbished in Liberia under LEC contract
  • Private sector refurbishment services for the general marketplace
