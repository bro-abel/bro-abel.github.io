Meters and Systems

LIBANGO provides revenue management and vending systems for LEC that include a smart meter platform. We supply and install both regular and smart metering products (AMR/AMI) for LEC and large customers.

A pre-financed pilot program launched the rollout of a smart meter network in Liberia, focusing on the Sinkor and Congo Town belt. Ten transformers were upgraded with data concentrator units and over 2,000 customers were connected and monitored. A smart medium voltage meter installed at the LW&SC water treatment plant in White Plains helped resolve billing disputes between LEC and LW&SC.

LIBANGO partners with meter manufacturers in Asia and South Africa, including Inhemeter (China) and Netvendor (South Africa), to supply meters and provide technical support.

Installation Services

LIBANGO supports rapid meter deployment with installation services across Monrovia. We also supply and install solar and AC streetlights for private clients and LEC, along with cables, MCCB/MCB breakers, and other connection materials.
